DUSSHERA CELEBRATION

Dusshera (pronounced Dhash-era) is an Indian festival that is observed on the tenth day (Dashami) of the month of Ashwin on the Hindu lunar calendar. It celebrates the defeat of the demon king Ravana by the Hindu god Rama, and signifies the triumph of good over evil. The epic Ramayana tells the story of Lord Rama who wins Sita for his wife, only to have her carried off by Ravana, the demon king of Lanka.

ABOUT ASHA

ASHA at Georgia Tech

Asha for Education is a philanthropic, fundraising organization whose mission is to catalyze socio-economic change in India through the education of underprivileged children. AshaGT is the student wing of Asha for education Atlanta - which is one of the 50+ chapters throughout USA. We support carefully selected NPOs from different parts of India and support their mission by providing monetary assistance. We are passionate about ensuring that the promise of education is delivered to those who need it the most and we would love to work with you if you feel the same way!
